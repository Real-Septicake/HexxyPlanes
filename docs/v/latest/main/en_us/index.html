                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: dwawddww

                  Planar Intrusion (plane →)

                  • Renders
                    • Palettes
                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: dwawdeewedwwqqqwwqq

                      Sends me to the provided demiplane. Costs 10 charged amethyst if not already in a demiplane, costing 5 dust otherwise.


                      Upon entering a plane, the two blocks on the spawn point are broken, as if by a player, and any fragile blocks are not dropped. The spawn point is signified by a purple tinted version of the blocks that make up the walls.
